Famous Places to Visit in Pushkar

  1. Pushkar Lake
    Considered one of the holiest lakes in India, it's believed to have been created by Lord Brahma himself. Pilgrims flock here to take ritual baths and offer prayers at its numerous ghats.

  2. Brahma Temple
    Dedicated to Lord Brahma, the creator of the universe according to Hindu mythology, this temple is one of the very few temples in the world dedicated to Brahma and is a significant pilgrimage site for Hindus.

  3. Savitri Temple
    Perched atop Ratnagiri Hill, this temple is dedicated to Goddess Savitri, the wife of Lord Brahma. It offers panoramic views of Pushkar town and the surrounding desert landscape.

  4. Pushkar Camel Fair
    Held annually in November, this vibrant cultural event attracts thousands of visitors and features colorful camel trading, folk music and dance performances, camel races, and various traditional competitions.

  5. Pushkar Bazaar
    A bustling market offering a plethora of goods ranging from traditional Rajasthani clothing and jewelry to handicrafts, souvenirs, and spices, providing visitors with a vibrant shopping experience amidst the town's lively atmosphere.
