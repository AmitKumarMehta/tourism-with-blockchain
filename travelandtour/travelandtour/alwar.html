Famous Places to Visit in Alwar

  1. Bala Quila (Alwar Fort)
    Dominating the skyline of Alwar, Bala Quila is a massive fort complex offering panoramic views of the city and surrounding Aravalli hills. It's steeped in history, with several palaces, temples, and reservoirs housed within its walls.

  2. Sariska Tiger Reserve
    A haven for wildlife enthusiasts, Sariska Tiger Reserve is home to Bengal tigers, leopards, wild boars, and numerous bird species. Visitors can enjoy safari rides through the lush forests, spotting wildlife amidst picturesque landscapes.

  3. City Palace
    Built by Maharaja Bakhtawar Singh, the City Palace is a splendid example of Rajput and Mughal architecture. It houses several ornate palaces, gardens, and museums, showcasing the grandeur of Alwar's royal heritage.

  4. Siliserh Lake Palace
    Situated on the banks of Siliserh Lake, this former royal palace is now a heritage hotel offering a serene retreat amidst scenic surroundings. Visitors can enjoy boating on the lake and explore the lush gardens and ancient ruins nearby.

  5. Vinay Vilas Mahal (Government Museum)
    Originally a royal palace, Vinay Vilas Mahal now houses the Government Museum, which exhibits a diverse collection of artifacts, including paintings, weapons, manuscripts, and sculptures, providing insights into Alwar's cultural and historical significance.
