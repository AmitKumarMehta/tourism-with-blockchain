Famous Places to Visit in Bikaner

  1. Junagarh Fort
    A magnificent fortress constructed in the 16th century, showcasing a blend of Rajput, Gujarati, and Mughal architectural styles, and housing several palaces, temples, and pavilions within its complex.

  2. Karni Mata Temple (Rat Temple):
    This unique temple is renowned for its population of sacred rats, believed to be the reincarnated descendants of Karni Mata's followers, attracting devotees and curious visitors alike.

  3. Lalgarh Palace and Museum
    A grand palace built in the Indo-Saracenic architectural style, housing a museum displaying a rich collection of artifacts, including royal memorabilia, weapons, paintings, and antique furniture.

  4. Gajner Palace and Lake
    Set amidst lush greenery and surrounded by the vast Thar Desert, Gajner Palace is a former hunting lodge of the Maharaja of Bikaner, offering a tranquil retreat with its scenic lake, wildlife sanctuary, and ornate architecture.

  5. National Research Centre on Camel
    Dedicated to the preservation and research of camels, this center offers insights into the importance of camels in the desert ecosystem and showcases various camel breeds, products, and research initiatives.
