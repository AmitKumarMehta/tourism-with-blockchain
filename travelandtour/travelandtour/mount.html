Famous Places to Visit in Mount Abu

  1. Dilwara Jain Temples
    Renowned for their exquisite marble architecture and intricate carvings, the Dilwara Jain Temples are a sacred pilgrimage site for Jains and a marvel of craftsmanship dating back to the 11th-13th centuries.

  2. Nakki Lake
    Surrounded by lush green hills, Nakki Lake is a serene and picturesque spot offering boating facilities amidst tranquil surroundings, making it a popular destination for both tourists and locals.

  3. Mount Abu Wildlife Sanctuary
    Home to a diverse range of flora and fauna, including rare species like the Indian leopard and sloth bear, the sanctuary offers opportunities for wildlife safaris, birdwatching, and nature walks amidst scenic landscapes.

  4. Guru Shikhar
    he highest peak in the Aravalli Range, Guru Shikhar offers breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys, with the serene ambiance of the nearby Guru Dattatreya Temple adding to its spiritual significance.

  5. Achalgarh Fort
    A historic fortress dating back to the 14th century, Achalgarh Fort is known for its ancient temples, including the Achaleshwar Mahadev Temple and Kantinath Jain Temple, along with the picturesque Mandakini Lake nestled within its premises.
